Taco Recipe

Description

This recipe will guide you to making tacos so good that they will make you think you are standing in downtown Monterrey.

Ingredients

  • ground beef
  • chili powder
  • salt
  • cumin
  • dried oregano
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • tomato sauce

Steps

  1. Cook ground beef
  2. Drain excess fat
  3. Season
  4. Simmer
  5. Fry tortillas
  6. shape tortillas
  7. Toppings
