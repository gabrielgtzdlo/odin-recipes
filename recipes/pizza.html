pizza recipe

Description

This delicious pizza will be made in the most delicious way possible. You'll never say you have had better than this because it is amazing.

Ingredients

  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Cornmeal (to help slide the pizza onto the pizza stone
  • Tomato sauce (smooth or pureed)
  • Firm mozzarella cheese, grated
  • Fresh soft mozzarella cheese, separated into small clumps
  • Fontina cheese, grated
  • Parmesan cheese, grated
  • Feta cheese, crumbled
  • Mushrooms, very thinly sliced if raw, otherwise first saut√©ed
  • Bell peppers, stems and seeds removed, very thinly sliced
  • Italian pepperoncini, thinly sliced
  • Italian sausage, cooked ahead and crumbled
  • Sliced black olives
  • Chopped fresh basil
  • Baby arugula, tossed in a little olive oil, added as pizza comes out of the oven
  • Pesto
  • Pepperoni, thinly sliced
  • Onions, thinly sliced raw or caramelized
  • Ham, thinly sliced

Steps

  1. Place pizza stone at bottom of oven.
  2. Preheat oven to 550 degrees.
  3. Prepare a work surface.
  4. Roll out the dough.
  5. Transfer the dough to a square of parchment paper.
  6. Par bake.
  7. Top pizza.
  8. Bake pizza.
  9. Remove pizza from oven.
